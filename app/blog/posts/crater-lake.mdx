Visited Crater Lake National Park before the Cleetwood Cove Trail closure. It's the only trail that goes down to the water!

The water was gorgeously blue and quite cold. I was suprised to learn that Crater Lake is essentially one big puddle. All the water is from rainfall and snowmelt.
